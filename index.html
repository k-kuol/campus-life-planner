About

What this is

Campus Life Planner is a small offline-first tool for tracking tasks and events a week throws at a student: classes, deadlines, meetings, and the small things that slip your mind. You jot down what you need to do, when it is due, and how long it will take, and it stays in one place so you can find it again later. Everything lives on your own device; there's nothing to sign up for.
